## Expertise & Specializations

### All Licence Types Mastered

Beverly is expert in all 10 types of liquor licence applications:

1. **New Liquor Licence Applications (Section 36)**
   - Restaurants, pubs, bars, taverns
   - Bottle stores and liquor shops
   - Hotels and guest houses
   - All business types

2. **Licence Transfers (Section 65)**
   - Business sale and purchase
   - Ownership changes
   - Due diligence for buyers
   - Expedited processing

3. **Temporary Licences (Section 48(1))**
   - Seasonal businesses
   - Pop-up establishments
   - Market traders
   - Trial periods

4. **Event Licences (Section 48(4))**
   - Weddings and receptions
   - Music festivals
   - Corporate events
   - Special occasions

5. **Premises Alterations (Section 53)**
   - Renovations and extensions
   - Layout changes
   - Expansion projects
   - Regularization of unauthorized changes

6. **Extended Trading Hours (Section 59)**
   - Late-night trading
   - Nightclubs and entertainment venues
   - Weekend extensions
   - Special trading periods

7. **Financial Interest Transfers (Section 51)**
   - Partnership changes
   - Shareholding adjustments
   - Adding/removing partners
   - Corporate restructuring

8. **Liquor Storage (Section 57)**
   - Warehouse applications
   - Off-site storage
   - Multiple locations
   - Bulk storage facilities

9. **Licence Removal (Section 66)**
   - Business relocations
   - Moving to new premises
   - Maintaining licence status
   - Strategic moves

10. **Trading Consent (Section 65(3))**
    - Immediate trading during transfers
    - Revenue protection
    - Business continuity
    - Fast-track processing
