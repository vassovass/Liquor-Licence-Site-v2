## FAQ Section

### Heading (H2)
**Frequently Asked Questions About Liquor Licences**

#### How long does a liquor licence application take in South Africa?
The timeline varies by licence type and province, but typically ranges from 60-120 days. With our expertise, we ensure the fastest possible processing time by submitting complete, error-free applications.

#### How much does a liquor licence cost in Cape Town?
Costs vary depending on the type of licence, premises size, and specific requirements. Contact us for a free consultation and accurate quote for your specific situation.

#### Can I apply for a liquor licence myself?
While it's legally possible, the process is complex with strict requirements. Most DIY applications face delays or rejection. Using an experienced consultant like Beverly Jeursen significantly increases your approval chances.

#### What types of businesses need a liquor licence?
Restaurants, pubs, taverns, bottle stores, hotels, guest houses, event venues, clubs, and any business selling alcohol to the public requires a liquor licence.

#### Do you handle liquor licence renewals?
Yes, we assist with all aspects of liquor licences including renewals, transfers, alterations, and new applications.
